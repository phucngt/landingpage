## 13. Safe Example KPI Definitions

First Login Rate:
Users who logged in for the first time / Accounts opened

Cash-in Rate:
Customers with cash-in / Accounts opened

Trading Conversion Rate:
Customers with at least one matched order / Accounts opened

Active Trading Customer:
Customer with trading value greater than 0 in the selected period

NAV Customer:
Customer with end-of-period NAV greater than 0

Dormant Customer:
Customer who previously logged in or traded but has no recent activity for a defined period

Broker Completion Score:
Completion Rate × Customer Volume Weight

Revenue Contribution:
Customer fee + margin interest, depending on available data
