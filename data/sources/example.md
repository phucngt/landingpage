## How does Phuc structure dashboards?

He treats every dashboard as a small product: clear KPI definitions at the
top, drilldowns one click away, and "what changed / why / who is impacted /
what to do" stitched into the layout so the page itself answers business
questions instead of just charting them.

## What's Phuc's typical engagement model?

Stakeholder interview → KPI tree on paper → mock dataset in SQL Server →
Power BI prototype within a week → iterate weekly with business users
until the report is opened daily without prompting.
